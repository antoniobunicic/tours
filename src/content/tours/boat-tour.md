Escape the city and take to the Adriatic for the ultimate Split experience from the water. Our small-group boat tour (maximum 10 passengers) ensures a personalized, unhurried journey through the stunning Central Dalmatian archipelago — one of the most spectacular island chains in the Mediterranean.

Departing from Split's iconic Riva waterfront, your captain — a Split native with decades on these waters — will navigate you to spots that can only be reached by sea. The highlight of every trip is the Blue Lagoon on Drvenik Mali, where the water shimmers between turquoise and emerald in a sheltered bay surrounded by pine-covered coastline. Drop anchor and jump in for a swim that you'll be talking about for years.

Snorkeling equipment is included for all guests, and the sea's remarkable clarity means you'll likely spot sea urchins, fish, and starfish just metres below the surface. Light snacks and refreshing drinks are served on board as you sail between islands and soak up the Dalmatian sun.

This tour runs year-round (weather permitting) and is equally magical at sunrise or in the late afternoon golden light. With a maximum of 10 guests, you'll never feel like you're part of a crowded excursion — just a small group of people sharing something truly special on the Adriatic.
